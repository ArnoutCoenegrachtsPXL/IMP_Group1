## 3. Information We Collect

### 3.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Account information:** Display name, email address, phone number, profile photo, location, pronouns, and bio.
- **Meter readings:** Electricity meter reading values, dates, and photographs you upload.
- **Communications:** Messages you send through our support system.
- **Preferences:** Your settings, feature selections, notification preferences, and energy configuration.

### 3.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data** (if you opt in): Pages visited, features used, clicks, and session duration — collected to improve the app.
- **Device information:** Browser type, operating system version, screen resolution, and language settings.
- **Log data:** IP address, date/time of access, error logs, and performance metrics.
- **Cookies:** Essential session cookies required for authentication. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

### 3.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Energy tariff data:** Published tariff schedules from Eskom and local municipalities (publicly available, not personal data).
- **Google Translate:** If you select a non-English language, page text is sent to Google's translation service. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

## 5. Data Sharing

We share your personal information only in limited circumstances:

- **Service providers:** Trusted vendors who help us deliver BrightBox (cloud hosting, email delivery, QR code generation). All vendors are bound by data processing agreements.
- **Legal compliance:** When required by law, court order, or government authority.
- **Business transfers:** In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with advance notice and the option to delete your account.
- **Anonymised data:** Aggregated, de-identified energy usage statistics may be shared with energy researchers. This is controlled by your Privacy settings.
- **Leaderboard:** If you enable public leaderboard display, your display name and energy score are visible to other users.

We **never** sell your personal data to advertisers or data brokers.

## 8. Security

We protect your data using:
- **Encryption in transit:** TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
- **Encryption at rest:** AES-256 for stored data
- **Access controls:** Role-based access; only authorised staff can access personal data
- **Two-factor authentication:** Available for all accounts (strongly recommended)
- **Regular security audits:** Annual penetration testing
- **Breach notification:** We will notify affected users and regulators within 72 hours of discovering a breach, as required by GDPR
